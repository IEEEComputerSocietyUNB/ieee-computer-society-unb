Turkish Pide

24-08-19 <p><strong>Ingredients</strong></p> <ul> <li>1 can (395g) sweetened condensed milk</li> <li>4 eggs</li> <li>120gm all purpose flour</li> <li>1/2 tblsp baking powder</li> <li>50gm unsalted butter, melted and cooled</li> <li>Icing sugar for dusting</li> </ul> <p><strong>Preparation</strong></p> <p>Preheat oven to 180C. Generously butter a 22cm ring cake pan.</p> <p>Place all ingredients in a large bowl and mix well. Pour the batter into prepared pan and bake for 20 minutes or until a skewer comes out clean. </p> <p>Remove from oven and place on a wire rack to cool completely before unmolding.</p> <p>Dust with icing sugar before serving.</p>
